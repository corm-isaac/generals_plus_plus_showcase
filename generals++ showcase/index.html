Generals++

Version: 0.1.1

What is Generals++?

Generals++ is a Hearts of Iron IV mod. This mod adds new unit leaders, field marshals, army/navy/air chiefs and military high command advisors from different wars and time periods from history. Additionally, Generals++ will also have the option to add generals from fictional works as well.

Currently this mod adds:

  • 48 Generals/Marshals
  • 8 Advisors
  • 33 Custom Portraits

Supported Settings

These are the current settings that are supported in the mod.

  • French Revolutionary Wars[1792-1802]
  • War of the Sixth Coalition[1813]
